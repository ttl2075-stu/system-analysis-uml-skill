# Artifact Scoring Rubric — system-analysis-uml

## Thang điểm
- 0: Không có / sai hoàn toàn
- 1: Có nhưng thiếu nghiêm trọng
- 2: Đủ tối thiểu
- 3: Tốt
- 4: Xuất sắc / dùng làm mẫu

## 1. Requirements Artifacts (20 điểm)
### 1.1 Actors & Scope (0–4)
- Ranh giới in-scope/out-of-scope rõ, không mơ hồ.
### 1.2 Use Case Catalog (0–4)
- UC đặt theo động từ nghiệp vụ, bao phủ actor chính.
### 1.3 Use Case Descriptions (0–4)
- Đủ pre/main/alt/exception/postconditions.
### 1.4 Business Rules Consistency (0–4)
- Không mâu thuẫn giữa UC.
### 1.5 Evidence Mapping (0–4)
- Có liên kết module/API/test.

## 2. Analysis Models (20 điểm)
### 2.1 Activity Quality (0–4)
### 2.2 Domain Model Coherence (0–4)
### 2.3 State Model Validity (0–4)
### 2.4 Structural-Behavioral Consistency (0–4)
### 2.5 Notation Correctness (0–4)

## 3. Design Models (20 điểm)
### 3.1 Package Boundaries (0–4)
### 3.2 Component Runtime Clarity (0–4)
### 3.3 Sequence Realism vs Architecture (0–4)
### 3.4 Data/Transaction Strategy (0–4)
### 3.5 Security/Error Design (0–4)

## 4. Traceability (20 điểm)
### 4.1 UC→Endpoint (0–4)
### 4.2 Endpoint→Service→Repo→Model (0–4)
### 4.3 Mapping tới Integration Test (0–4)
### 4.4 Coverage of Core Flows (0–4)
### 4.5 Gap Tracking Quality (0–4)

## 5. Operability & Maintainability (20 điểm)
### 5.1 Readability & Structure (0–4)
### 5.2 Terminology Consistency (0–4)
### 5.3 Update Process Defined (0–4)
### 5.4 Runtime Rules Compliance (0–4)
### 5.5 Reviewer Actionability (0–4)

## Xếp loại tổng
- 90–100: Production-ready
- 75–89: Strong internal
- 60–74: Usable with gaps
- <60: Needs major rework
